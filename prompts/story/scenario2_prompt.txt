- The moment people first notice the towers
- The ground shaking during their appearance (felt physically by characters)
- News spreading rapidly through phones, TV, and social media
- Public confusion, curiosity, and speculation

FOR EACH OF THE 5 MAIN CHARACTERS:

- Show where they are when the ground begins to shake
- Show their immediate physical and emotional reaction to the shaking
- Show how they first see or hear about the towers
- Include at least one natural interaction or conversation with others about it

CONSTRAINTS:

- The 5 main characters MUST remain the same characters already established
- DO NOT create new main characters
- Side/background people may exist but must not become new focal characters
- No supernatural explanation is revealed
- No danger or combat occurs
- The towers are only observed, not interacted with

WORLD STATE:

- Life continues, but is briefly disrupted by the shaking
- After the shaking stops, daily routines resume with heightened attention on the towers

TONE:

- Initial surprise from the ground shaking
- Followed by curiosity and unease
