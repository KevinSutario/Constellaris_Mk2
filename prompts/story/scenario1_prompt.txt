SCENARIO:
- The story takes place in modern-day Seoul
- Follow EXACTLY 5 main characters living separate lives
- Each character goes through part of their normal daily routine
- Their lives do NOT intersect yet
- No unusual or supernatural events occur

CONSTRAINTS:
- No fantasy elements
- No mention of towers or anything unnatural
- No characters meet each other yet
- No major events or disruptions
- Do NOT skip time abruptly
- Do NOT summarize (everything must be shown through scenes)

CHARACTER REQUIREMENTS:
- All 5 characters MUST appear in this chapter
- Each character must:
  - perform at least one clear action
  - have at least one internal thought or emotional reaction
  - have at least one small interaction (dialogue or social moment)

- Characters must feel DISTINCT through:
  - behavior
  - dialogue tone
  - decisions
  - reactions to small situations

FLOW:
- The narrative must feel continuous and seamless
- No sections, labels, or visible structure
- Transitions between characters must feel natural and unannounced
- Scenes should blend like a film

FOCUS:
- Grounded daily life (school, work, commuting, environment)
- Subtle emotional tension beneath normal routines
- Small frustrations, dissatisfaction, or quiet unease
- Environmental immersion (sounds, movement, surroundings)

TONE:
- Calm but slightly heavy
- Something feels "off" emotionally, but nothing is visibly wrong
- Each character should feel like something is missing in their life

ENDING:
- End with a subtle shift in atmosphere
- A quiet sense of unease or tension should linger
- DO NOT introduce any fantasy or explanation
