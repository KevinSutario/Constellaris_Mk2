SCENARIO:

- A person enters one of the towers and becomes global news

EVENTS TO INCLUDE:

- Media coverage spreading rapidly (TV broadcasts, phone alerts, online discussions)
- Public attention intensifies as people follow the event in real time

PROGRESSION:

- The person entering the tower is initially treated as curiosity or bravery
- Then it is confirmed that the person has died inside the tower

FOR EACH OF THE 5 MAIN CHARACTERS:

- Show how they first learn about this event (TV, phone, conversation, etc.)
- Show their immediate and personal reaction:
  fear, dismissal, curiosity, indifference, skepticism
- Include at least one natural conversation with others about it
- Show differing opinions and perspectives

CONSTRAINTS:

- The 5 main characters MUST remain the same characters already established
- DO NOT create new main characters
- The person who entered the tower must remain anonymous or distant (not a new character focus)
- No direct interaction with the tower occurs
- No supernatural explanation is revealed

WORLD STATE:

- Society becomes more alert and divided in opinion
- Some people become fearful, others dismiss it or rationalize it
- Daily life continues, but attention toward the towers increases significantly

TONE:

- Growing unease
- Subtle fear begins to emerge
- Emotional contrast between different characters

IMPORTANT:

- Focus on reactions, dialogue, and thoughts
- Do NOT introduce new major events beyond this incident
- Do NOT resolve anything
